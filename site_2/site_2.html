Bio


The rise and fall of a music band

Pretty Vicious was a Welsh rock band originally from Merthyr Tydfil. The band signed to Big Machine Records in 2018, and their new single "Move" and "Are You Entertained" were released under the label. Their debut album Beauty of Youth was released on 12 July 2019.

The music "Are you ready for me" was featured in the game "Life is Strange: Before the Storm". The band was under the name Firewalk.

In a statement issued 23 October 2019, the band announced that they would be splitting up and then re-emerge as a new group in 2020, without frontman Brad Griffiths, who took a break from performances after health issues happened.

Their last show took place at 17 January 2020.

Pretty Vicious said they will re-surface in 2020 like a phoenix from the ashes under a new guise and with another singer.
